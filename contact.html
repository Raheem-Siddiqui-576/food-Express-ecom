Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?
You can track your order in real-time from the "My Orders" section after logging into your account. You'll also receive SMS and email updates.
What is your refund policy?
If your order is incorrect or damaged, we offer full refunds within 24 hours. Contact our support team with your order details.
How can I become a delivery partner?
Visit our "Careers" page and fill out the delivery partner application form. We'll get back to you within 3-5 business days.
Do you offer bulk orders for events?
Yes! We offer special discounts for bulk orders. Please email us at catering@foodexpress.com with your requirements.
